Beloved Women of the Turning,

Thank you for saying yes to stepping into this first circle gathering. This is an opening \- a living field where we come not as roles or titles, but as whole women attuning to what wants to emerge through us and through this time of great turning. As shared in our letter of intent, this space is a peer ceremonial container devoted to coherence, relational leadership, and service to life. We come together to listen, learn, and co-create solutions. Below are a few gentle invitations to help prepare.

**Before We Gather**

* Please review our container agreements here: \[link\]  
* Share your bio if you haven’t yet, and read others’ bios here: \[link\]  
* Join the Signal group for shared communication: \[link\]

**What to Bring**

* An item for our shared altar \- e.g. a poem, crystal, object, guide, or symbol that carries meaning. We’ll be invited to share a few words about why we chose it, and / or to respond to the reflection prompt shared below.  
* A cup of water (ideally from a fresh local water source) for a shared practice   
* Something to drink for yourself  
* A candle to light during our time together  
* Pen & paper for reflections  
* Your presence \- and a willingness to step into a portal together

**Optional Reflection Prompt**

You may wish to sense into this beforehand:   
*How do you see our world after the great turning? What is nourished through divine feminine leadership? What unfolds over the next 25 years \- and how do we bring that healing into the present?*

**Our Time Together \- High-Level Flow**

* Opening blessing  
* Container agreements/intentions for creating this community  
* Brief check-in \- how are you arriving?  
* Shared altar offerings and/or reflections on the prompt  
* Co-creating our shared theory of change  
* Ideas for upcoming gatherings  
* Questions, feedback, ideas, and sensing  
* Closing & next steps

This community is not centered around any one organization or initiative \- it is a space to remember, listen, and amplify one another as women walking in service to life.

Thank you for bringing your presence, your wisdom, and your willingness to co-create something tender and powerful together.

With love and anticipation,
