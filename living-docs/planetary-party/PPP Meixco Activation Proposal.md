# **🌎 Planetary Party Protocol Event Proposal**

## **Mexico Activation | Executive Summary**

This proposal recommends that we develop a full activation cycle / community engagement process in **Veracruz, Mexico**, beginning with a 4–5 day Activation Gathering at **Huerto Roma Verde in Mexico City**, followed by a 6-month activation process in Coatepec, culminating in the first Planetary Party Veracruz.

This will serve as a proof of concept and replicable model for future bioregional activations.

# **1\. Core Challenges We Are Facing**

At this stage, Planetary Party is experiencing:

* Diffused focus across multiple bioregions  
* No clearly defined first milestone  
* A team-wide feeling of “we are not ready yet”  
* Concern about insufficient funding  
* No completed activation cycle to demonstrate credibility

The narrative “we need more funding before we begin” is slowing action.

However, activation itself could be the mechanism that generates funding, partnerships, and credibility.

I believe we need at least one focused, strategically viable bioregion to demonstrate the model.

# **2\. Strategic Focus: Veracruz, Mexico**

### **Why Mexico?**

Operational Readiness:

* Deep existing relationships in Coatepec and Veracruz  
* Ongoing conversations with local community leaders  
* Two Mexican core team members  
* Language fluency and cultural fluency  
* Low logistical barriers

Political & Institutional Opportunity:

* The governor of Mexico is receptive to regenerative and cultural innovation initiatives  
* Paco Ayala, founder of Huerto Roma Verde, has strong local and governmental relationships  
* Huerto Roma Verde has institutional legitimacy and visibility

Strategic Positioning:

* Mexico City is a cultural and intellectual hub of Latin America  
* Spanish-speaking ecosystem access  
* Strong regenerative and urban agriculture movement already active

This is activating where infrastructure already exists.

# **3\. Phase 1: Activation Gathering at Huerto Roma Verde**

Location: Huerto Roma Verde, Mexico City  
Duration: 4–5 days  
Format: Public ticketed event (w/ work trade opps & scholarships) \+ curated invitation

This event functions as:

* Celebration  
* Knowledge transfer  
* Strategic lab  
* Fundraising launch  
* Recruitment hub  
* Cultural convergence

# **4\. Why Huerto Roma Verde**

Huerto Roma Verde is a living regenerative ecosystem that models:

* Community governance structures  
* Social enterprise “cells”  
* Urban farming systems  
* Soil regeneration practices  
* Cultural programming  
* Healing arts  
* Economic coordination  
* Public trust

The gathering will:

* Document their model  
* Extract replicable principles  
* Honor and give back to Huerto  
* Learn from their governance structure  
* Create knowledge transfer sessions

This positions Huerto Roma Verde as a teacher to the world while activating Veracruz.

# **5\. Structure of the 4–5 Day Activation**

### **Day 1: The Ecosystem Model**

Focus:

* Governance systems  
* Financial ecosystem  
* Social enterprise cell structure  
* Decision-making processes  
* Economic interdependence

Outcome: Documentation of the Huerto model.

### **Day 2: The Builders**

Integration with Permatours & other aligned orgs:

* Hands-on regenerative builds  
* Soil regeneration workshops  
* Chinampas visit  
* Applied ecological design  
* Builder network activation

Outcome: Permatours arm of Planetary Party integrated into activation protocol.

### **Day 3: Veracruz Strategy Lab**

Community leaders from Coatepec present:

* Cloud forest degradation  
* Water extraction crisis  
* Youth engagement needs  
* Local regenerative economic opportunities

Participants form working groups around defined problem areas.

Outcome:Defined pilot projects and solution frameworks.

### **Day 4: Cultural Convergence & Commitment**

* Ecstatic dance  
* Music and arts  
* Healing offerings  
* Ceremony  
* Commitment circle  
* Public launch of Flow Fund Veracruz

Outcome: Emotional cohesion \+ formal commitment \+ funding launch.

# **6\. Clear Goals & Key Performance Indicators**

The activation must have measurable outcomes.

### **Financial KPIs**

* Raise $50,000 initial seed capital (target baseline)  
* Secure anchor donors  
* Launch Flow Fund Veracruz legally and structurally

### **Participation KPIs**

* 150–300 attendees  
* 20+ international / intergenerational changemakers  
* 10 long-term collaborators willing to embed in Veracruz  
* 5 institutional partners engaged

### **Programmatic KPIs**

* Define 3–5 pilot projects  
* Establish working groups with leads  
* Produce a documented Planetary Party Activation Protocol

### **Media KPIs**

* Full documentation of activation  
* 10+ high-quality media assets  
* Clear narrative for donor outreach

# **7\. Fundraising Strategy**

The activation is itself the fundraising engine.

Revenue Streams:

1. Ticket Sales  
   * Tiered pricing  
   * Scholarship model  
2. Donor Sponsorship  
   * Anchor donors invited to closed strategy sessions  
   * Naming opportunities for Flow Fund Veracruz  
3. Institutional Partnerships  
   * Huerto Roma Verde network  
   * Government relationships via Paco  
4. Post-Event Campaign  
   * “Activate Veracruz” donor campaign  
   * Story-driven fundraising using event documentation

Funding is tied to defined pilot projects, not vague vision.

# **8\. 6-Month Veracruz Activation**

Following the gathering:

Month 1–2:

* Working groups formalized  
* Flow Fund structure operational  
* Pilot project budgets defined

Month 3–4:

* First capital deployed  
* Permatours builds begin  
* International volunteers embedded

Month 5–6:

* Project milestones achieved  
* Impact documented  
* Regional narrative strengthened

Month 6: Planetary Party Veracruz

Grounded in:

* Capital deployment  
* Community integration  
* Visible impact

# **9\. Milestones Timeline**

Month 0: Team approval \+ Huerto confirmation

Month 1: Dates confirmed \+ budget drafted

Month 2: Speaker invitations \+ donor outreach begins

Month 3: Activation Gathering

Month 9: Planetary Party Veracruz

# **10\. Replication Framework**

The Mexico Activation becomes a prototype.

1. Identify bioregion with strong partner  
2. Gather at existing regenerative hub  
3. Learn from functioning ecosystem  
4. Activate working groups  
5. Launch Flow Fund  
6. 6-month build cycle  
7. Culminate in Planetary Party  
8. Document and export model

This becomes exportable to Jamaica, Cascadia, Colombia, the Northeast, & beyond.

The Planetary Party Protocol does not need global expansion right now. It needs a successful activation cycle. Veracruz offers:

* Political openness  
* Existing community partnerships  
* Cultural fluency  
* Infrastructure  
* Network density  
* Narrative strength
