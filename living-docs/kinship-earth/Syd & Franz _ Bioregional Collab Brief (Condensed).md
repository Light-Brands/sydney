**1. Permatours - 40+ Land Hubs Ready for DMRV**

40+ community hubs across the Northeast are doing regenerative agriculture, food forests, natural building, and ecological restoration - but lack data infrastructure to prove their impact or access carbon finance.

**Where we intersect:**

* DMRV tools to document and verify ecological outcomes (soil carbon, biodiversity, water retention)
* Digital twins for mapping and tracking regeneration over time
* Carbon credit access - pooling verified data across 40+ sites for Article 6 or voluntary markets
* Collective data infrastructure for pooling land data and accessing additional capital

Permatours is also developing a dual-token system (utility + voice) and a chapters model for bioregional replication - your tokenomics infrastructure could accelerate this.

**Readiness**: High. Community is active, hubs exist, relationships are strong. Needs a funded data collection role.

**2. Kinship Earth - Digital Infrastructure for Bioregional Flow Funds**

Kinship Earth deploys unrestricted flow funding across 12+ bioregions and funds bioregional activation series (NE launching Spring 2026, Mexico planned for Summer 2026), co-created with Permatours, Bioregional Embassy, and the wider Planetary Party ecosystem.

**The gap:** Funders want proof of impact without burdening grassroots communities with heavy reporting. DAFs and foundations have compliance needs that don't map easily onto trust-based flow funding. Communities starting their own flow funds need simple but credible tools.

**Digital infrastructure we're exploring:**

* **Pooled Funds (Vaults)** - themed digital flow funds with transparent tracking funders can verify independently
* **Commitments** - turning funding promises into verifiable pledges communities can plan around
* **Vested Escrow** - steady disbursement streams instead of lump-sum uncertainty
* **Impact Credentials (Hypercerts + Attestations)** - lightweight updates verified by independent observers, creating permanent records without lengthy grant reports

**Where we intersect:**

* Donor-facing tokens with voice in funding allocation
* Flow fund management tools bridging diverse capital sources (DAFs, family offices, foundations, crypto)
* Reporting/verification layer that satisfies institutions while staying trust-based
* Polycentric governance tools for collective funding decisions

**Active bioregions:**

* Northeast (Turtle Island) - first activation series underway
* Mexico - forming around Huerto Roma Verde and the Cloud Forest bioregion
* Colombia - 5+ bioregional nodes active
* Jamaica - Rastafari Indigenous Village and Montego Bay hubs
* Guatemala - Indigenous-led flow fund around Lake Atitlan
* East Africa - coastal forest women's flow fund across five countries (via Regenerosity)
* Cascadia - 8 landscape hubs coordinating at bioregional scale

**Readiness**: Digital infrastructure play is baked into the NE activation, already in motion. Fundraising needed for broader buildout.
