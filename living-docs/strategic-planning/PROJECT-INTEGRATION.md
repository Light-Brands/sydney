## Role Clarity Framework

To avoid burnout and boundary confusion, use this framework:

| Role | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Lead** | Makes final decisions, sets direction, accountable for outcomes |
| **Partner** | Active collaborator, shares in design and execution |
| **Contributor** | Provides specific skills or support, doesn't drive direction |
| **Supporter** | Emotional support, sounding board, occasional help |

| Project | Syd's Role | Eslerh's Role |

| Kinship Earth | Lead | Contributor (media) |
| Planetary Party | Partner (structure) | Partner (culture) |

| Unified Sanctuaries | Partner | Contributor (media, arts) |
| Live with Syd | Lead | Contributor (guest, media) |
| Land (personal) | Partner | Partner |

## Partnership Health Practices

Working and living together requires intentional care:

### Boundaries to Maintain
- Designate work-free time and spaces (meals, evenings, one day/week)
- Syd's projects are not Eslerh's obligation, and vice versa
- Financial compensation for cross-project work (Micelio billing Kinship Earth, etc.)
- Each person maintains their own creative and professional identity

### Rhythms to Establish
- Weekly partnership check-in (life, not just work)
- Monthly strategic review (projects, finances, priorities)
- Quarterly planning session (using these strategic documents)
- Annual retreat for long-term vision and life design

### Support Structures
- Each person has their own community and friendships beyond the partnership
- Access to mentors, advisors, or coaches outside the relationship
- Willingness to hire help rather than overloading the partnership
