## Where Your Tech Meets Our Communities

### 1. Permatours -- 30+ Land Hubs Ready for DMRV and Carbon Credit Access

**What's happening:** Permatours has built a network of 40+ land-based community hubs across the Northeast doing regenerative agriculture, natural building, food forests, and ecological restoration. These are farms, ecovillages, cooperatives, and community land projects run by people who show up and build with their hands.

**The gap:** We haven't done a great job at data collection. We haven't tested soil before and after we install a hugelkultur garden or plant a food forest. These communities are generating real ecological value but can't prove it, can't access carbon finance, and can't pool their impact into something fundable.

**Where you come in:**
- **DMRV tools** to help these hubs document and verify ecological outcomes (soil carbon, biodiversity, water retention)
- **Digital twins** of land projects so communities can map, plan, and track regeneration over time
- **Carbon credit access** -- pooling verified data across 30+ sites to access Article 6 frameworks or voluntary markets
- **Collective data infrastructure** -- helping these lands pool data and potentially hold land together in a trust to access additional capital

**What's emerging:** Permatours is shifting toward a mutual aid model with a dual-token system (utility tokens + voice tokens) and a chapters model for replication in other bioregions. Your tokenomics infrastructure could accelerate this.

**Readiness:** High for NE bioregion. Community is active, hubs exist, relationships are strong. Needs a dedicated person for data collection and DMRV onboarding.

### 2. Kinship Earth -- Digital Infrastructure for Bioregional Flow Funds

**What's happening:** Kinship Earth deploys unrestricted flow funding to trusted grassroots leaders across 12+ bioregions. We're also facilitating bioregional activation series -- supporting communities in preparing to launch their own flow funds and tap into local wealth.

**The gap:** We need digital infrastructure that helps communities manage flow funds (both traditional and crypto), provides transparency for donors and DAFs, and supports lightweight verification and reporting without burdening grassroots leaders.

**Where you come in:**
- **Donor-facing infrastructure** that gives contributors a token representing their participation, with voice in funding allocation decisions (the model Susanna Cho and I have been exploring with Bioregional Embassy)
- **Flow fund management tools** -- helping bioregional communities attract, receive, and steward capital from diverse sources (DAFs, family offices, foundations, crypto)
- **Reporting and verification layer** -- appealing to institutional funders while keeping the process trust-based and lightweight
- **Polycentric governance tools** for communities making collective funding decisions

**Active bioregions that could benefit:**
- **Northeast of Turtle Island** -- first bioregional activation series launching now
- **Mexico** -- activation forming around Puerto Roma Verde (eco-village in Mexico City), Cloud Forest bioregion, and other hubs connected through Juan Carlos and Esler
- **Colombia** -- 5+ bioregional nodes active (Aguaclara, Sinu, Sierra Nevada de Santa Marta, Bogota, Cali)
- **Jamaica** -- partnership with Rastafari Indigenous Village in Montego Bay
- **Guatemala** -- Indigenous-led work around Lake Atitlan
- **East Africa** -- coastal forest women's flow fund across five countries
- **Cascadia** -- 8 landscape hubs coordinating regeneration at bioregional scale

**Readiness:** Medium-high. Activation series are underway. Fundraising cycle needed to resource digital infrastructure buildout.

### 3. Planetary Party -- Bioregional Activation as a Trojan Horse

**What's happening:** The Planetary Party is a coordination protocol -- not another organization -- that helps communities move through cycles of sensing, mapping, flowing resources, celebrating, and regenerating. We do bioregional activation series that culminate in "peak festival" moments, building lasting positive-trace infrastructure along the way.

**The convergence with your work:**
- **Bioregional activation series as deployment sites** for your digital twin and governance tools -- each activation involves mapping, relationship building, governance education, and eventually launching bioregional tokens and flow funds
- **The NE bioregional token** -- we're developing tokenomics for the Northeast bioregion that could integrate with your infrastructure
- **Festival as activation Trojan horse** -- you said it yourself. People come with open minds, the culture stays after the event. Our activations are designed to produce lasting infrastructure (gardens, governance models, water systems) with each cycle
- **Storytelling and use case documentation** -- every activation is documented. We can tell the shared story of what these tools make possible in real communities

**Your Burning Man connection:** A lot of our Permatours community members are burners. The Planetary Party narrative is: instead of burning down epic sculptures, we build lasting infrastructure. There's natural overlap with the pan-European burn activation you're planning with Interlaken and the European Leadership Summit. Happy to connect you with Athena Demos, who does digital twin work for the Burning Man community.

**Readiness:** Active across 5+ bioregions. The activation series format is the ideal deployment context for your tools.

### 4. Unified Sanctuaries -- A Living Lab for Everything

**What's happening:** We're acquiring land in Southern Vermont to build a regenerative village with four autonomous pillars (farm, retreat center, event venue, community living). The whole thing is designed as an open-source blueprint.

**Where you come in:**
- **Digital twin** of the entire village -- plan in the digital sphere, reverse engineer into physical reality, track development over time
- **Polycentric governance in practice** -- four autonomous nodes with shared infrastructure, consent-based decision-making, rotating leadership
- **Tokenomics** -- utility tokens for the circular economy, voice tokens for governance, trust units for contribution-based equity that people can liquidate when they leave (solving the "I want land but can't afford it" problem)
- **Carbon and ecological verification** for the permaculture farm and land restoration

**Readiness:** Pre-acquisition / planning phase. Governance and tokenomics design underway.
