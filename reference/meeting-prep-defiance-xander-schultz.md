## What Is DEFIANCE.org

- A **non-profit social impact club** (not a 501c3 -- intentionally structured for speed and candor)
- Mission: centralized hub for democratic action -- "a Google for civic engagement"
- Membership model: $1-$1,000/month, members vote on which "missions" to fund
- Does NOT fund candidates, PACs, or political campaigns
- Programs include:
  - **DEFIANCE Radio** -- daily live broadcast at 7:30am ET ("Dispatches for the Defiant")
  - **Weekly Spotlight** -- featuring national figures
  - **Private monthly briefings** with member voting on priorities
  - **DEFIANCE.News** (Substack) -- urgent reporting on threats to democracy
  - **Weekly Coffee** -- interactive member sessions
  - Partnerships with orgs like **Democracy Defenders Fund** for legal challenges
- Backed by high-profile supporters: Robert De Niro, Mark Ruffalo, Stacey Abrams, Jim Acosta, Ron Wyden

## Synergies & Alignment

### 1. Shared DNA: Decentralized Movement Building
- Both ecosystems reject centralized, extractive models in favor of distributed coordination
- DEFIANCE = "hub of resistance" with member-driven mission selection
- Planetary Party Protocol = federated bioregional coordination with consent-based governance
- **Overlap:** Both are building infrastructure for people to self-organize around shared values

### 2. Trust-Based, People-Powered Funding
- Kinship Earth's Flow Funding model (invitation-only, trust-based, communities control capital) vs. DEFIANCE's member-voted mission funding

- **Opportunity:** Cross-pollinate funding models -- Kinship Earth's bioregional flow funding could inform DEFIANCE's mission funding, and vice versa

### 3. Media & Storytelling as Movement Infrastructure
