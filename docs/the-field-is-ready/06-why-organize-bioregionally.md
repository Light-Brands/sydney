# Why Organize Bioregionally

[< Previous: What is the Planetary Party?](05-what-is-the-planetary-party.md) | [Back to Index](README.md) | [Next: Origin Story >](07-origin-story.md)

---

I want to explain why bioregions are central to this vision. If bioregions can effectively coordinate locally and cross-bioregionally, then parallel pathways are created to solve unsolved challenges of the current political and economic system.

Bioregionalism is not a replacement for governments, but a living complement to them as a distributed, place-based governance layer rooted in land, culture, trust, and care. The Planetary Party points toward a deeper architecture - one that merges the balance of masculine and feminine principles, e.g. those of action and care, structure and emergence, leadership and collective wisdom.
