## What This Is

These are recurring, co-creative live sessions exploring:

- Regenerative capital flows
- Bioregional coordination & governance
- Regenerative land stewardship
- Healing, heart sharing, and collective resilience

**This is not a webinar. This is a living, relational field.**

## How It Works

1. **Register for a Session** — All participants register in advance to help maintain a safe, respectful container.
2. **Receive Your Access Link** — You'll receive a private session link via email before the event.
3. **Join the Live Room** — You can participate with camera on or off, speaking or listening, chat or voice.

## Participation Flow

- Everyone is muted on entry
- Raise your hand or use chat to request to speak
- You'll be invited to unmute when it's your turn

## Community Agreements

By joining, you agree to uphold shared values of care, consent, and respect. See the full [Community Agreements](09-community-agreements.md).

## Ways to Engage

- Attend and listen
- Share reflections or questions
- Propose a topic or teaching
- Join as a future guest
- Help shape what emerges

## Weekly Themes

### Mondays — Regenerative Capital Flows
Unlocking resources for grassroots communities. Trust-based philanthropy. Flow Funding. Decentralized finance for regeneration.

### Wednesdays — Planetary Party
Bioregional coordination. Governance. Culture. Collective intelligence. Regenerative finance.

### Fridays — Unified Sanctuaries
Land stewardship. Community ownership. Trust models. Regenerative villages.

### Heart Sharing + Grounding Circles (monthly)
Non-recorded sacred space for emotional processing, regulation, and collective care.

## Consent & Recording

- Most sessions are recorded and published for broader learning.
- Heart Sharing circles are always private and unrecorded.
