**Women of the Turning**

**Letter of Intent**

Women have gathered for generations in service to life by tuning into the womb as the creative life force; embodying a strategic operating system for infrastructure through *rooted* body-based feminine gathering spaces. The intention for this circle is to sync as women in our collective fields of influence; create coherence and rhythmically weave the web our shared world needs right now. We are already moving in service to life. Now how can we amplify our reach? 

Our planet needs female leaders to step up NOW, attune, amplify and encourage one another, so we may create nodes of coherence all over the planet, ready to build from the rubble of this collapsing crumbling extractive empire. The women invited to these sessions are those we believe have the capacity to amplify one another’s missions and be the women the world needs, to continue to rise into leadership over the next decade. We are looking to explore how support for inner clarity and collective coherence, in attunement with Earth’s natural cycles, can help move intention into tangible, meaningful, coherent action. 

This circle is an emerging practice where we explore somatic practices for more embodied expressions of our voices. Where we will support shared learning in personal growth, the letting go of scarcity and victimhood, and practices for greater efficacy in establishing infrastructure in service to all life, at this collective liberation edge. 

We sense the urgency of this moment. In our first, introductory meeting, we will invite everyone to share their dreams and visions, the rituals and rites of passages we want to see more of, and what feels exciting as we come into this planetary turning of the age. 

We are thrilled to feel into the potential of this group and will honor what emerges to make these gatherings as meaningful, impactful, and potent as possible. It will not center any one organization, fund, or initiative \- rather, it will serve as a peer space for thought leaders to gather in ceremony. As women in this movement collaborating across all different sectors, we want the women we know to know each other.. and for each of us to bring our voices and impact; for our magic to be amplified in global community.

Our vision for the structure:

* 1 introductory online meeting to meet one another and align intentions (end of Feb)  
* 4 seasonal, summit-style online gatherings aligned with solstices and equinoxes  
* Annual, in-person convenings  
* Opportunities to join smaller, more concentrated cohorts between the 4 summits 

We require no sign-ups \- just the willingness to weave. The first introductory gathering will be held after the start of Lunar New Year \- towards the end of February, followed by a collective water blessing focused around the Spring Equinox. If this convening community sparks your interest \- please reply back letting me know. I would love to send you an official invitation.

We would also love to send you an invitation by post for the Spring Equinox gathering. If you feel comfortable, you are welcome to include your mailing address in your response. (It will be used solely for sending this invitation and for no other purpose). If sharing your address does not feel aligned for you, we will send your invitation by email.

**Lineage & Learning Being Brought to the Circle**

This circle will be shaped by the lived experience each participant brings. 

The spirit of this circle draws from living lineages of relational, trust-based giving and feminine wisdom, including Marion Rockefeller Weber’s Flow Funding and Susan Davis Moora’s KINS strategy. Marion’s way of gathering emphasizes trust, intuition, and relationship \- inviting spirit into the circle and allowing symbols to reveal what wants to emerge. Both of these women’s styles inform the spirit, pacing, and tone of how we convene \- honoring intuition, right relationship, and Earth as a living teacher. We’re beginning with a small, intentional community grounded in sharing, learning, and ceremony to allow for a tone *rooted* in feminine approaches. 

If you are inspired to join this community, **we warmly invite you to reply with a few sentences about who you are, what you are most passionate about, and what would feel most soul-fulfilling for you to experience / co-create within this circle.** We’ll share these descriptions with the group that will be joining, so we can all begin to get a sense of one another before we gather.

**Core Field Agreements**

*Purpose of the Field \- Allowance like Earth*

This circle is a relational field of practice devoted to the stewardship of life.

We gather as women to remember, restore, and re-embody THE mode of leadership the Earth system is inviting humanity into during these times; rooted in care, coherence, reciprocity, and responsibility to future generations. We are inviting coherence around our theory of change as women of the great turning, and devotional practice of it together.

This is a living field grounded in trust, presence, and service to all life. By entering this field, we agree to tend not only what we do together, but ***how*** **we are together**.

**1\. Relationship Before Outcome**

We prioritize relationship, listening, and attunement over urgency, strategy, or results.

We allow trust to form at its own pace, knowing that coherence is the foundation from which meaningful action may arise.

**2\. Presence, Honesty & Humanity**

We arrive as whole humans, not as roles, brands, titles, or representatives.

We speak from lived experience rather than abstraction.  
 We listen without preparing responses. We make space for silence, emotion, and not-knowing.

This field values truth over polish and authenticity over expertise.

**3\. Consent, Agency & Choice**

Participation is always by choice. You may pass, pause, or step back at any time.  
 No advising, fixing, or redirecting unless explicitly invited.  
 Each person is responsible for honoring their own capacity and boundaries.

Care includes respecting our own limits and those of others.

**4\. Stewardship & Non-Extraction**

This field exists in service to life, not individual agendas.

What emerges here is not to be extracted, claimed, or repurposed elsewhere without care or consent. Language, insights, or collective wisdom are not individual intellectual property.

If something wants to move outward, we name and steward it together.

**5\. Confidentiality & Field Integrity**

Confidentiality is essential to trust.

Personal stories, identities, and experiences shared in this space remain protected and are not to be repeated outside the circle without explicit permission.

At the same time, the *essence* of what moves through the field \- learnings, reflections, and non-attributed wisdom \- may ripple outward in ways that honor the collective and do not identify individuals. We distinguish between **field-level wisdom** and **person-specific information**, and protect the latter with care.

**6\. Integrity of Resources & Power**

This is not a fundraising, pitching or prospecting space. Money, resources, and influence are held with care and consciousness within this field. Any conversations related to funding, allocation, or financial stewardship occur only in explicitly named and consented sub-spaces. 

This agreement exists so all participants may engage with openness, ease, and trust.

**\~ Closing Intention \~**

These agreements are not rules, but guardians.

They exist to protect what is tender, powerful, and emergent \-   
and to create a field where wisdom, responsibility, and love for life can move freely,   
without pressure, coercion, or extraction.

By entering this circle, we agree to tend this field together, in integrity, humility, and shared stewardship.
