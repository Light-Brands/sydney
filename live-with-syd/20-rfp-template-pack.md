## Part 5 — Role Combination Guidance

In early stage, prioritize people who can hold two roles:

| Combo Role | Covers |
|-----------|--------|
| Tech Architect + Automation | Full stack build |
| Moderator + Community Manager | Live + Discord |
| Content Producer + Streaming | Media layer |
| Guest Liaison + Program Curator | Programming |
| Administrator + Fundraising | Ops + money |
